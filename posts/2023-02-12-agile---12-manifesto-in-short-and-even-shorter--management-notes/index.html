Agile - 12 manifesto in short and even shorter | Management Notes

Posted by Phong To on Sun, Feb 12, 2023

The Agile Manifesto consists of 12 principles for software development and project management. It could be understood generally but too hard to remember, so that I would like to rewrite them with my understanding in just several words:

12 Agile manifesto in short

1. Customer satisfaction is on top through early and continuous delivery

2. Welcome any changes, even late

3. Frequent delivery running software

4. Business people and developer collaboration every day

5. Motivated individuals, supportive and trust them to get the job done

6. Face-to-face conversation

7. Working software as primary measure of progress

8. Sustainable development to maintain a constant pace indefinitely

9. Continuous technical excellence and good design enhances

10. Simplicity is essential

11. Self-organizing creates best practices

12. Regular Retro for development process improvement
